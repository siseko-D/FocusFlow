About FocusFlow

CogniTask

What is FocusFlow?

FocusFlow is a powerful productivity application designed to help you organize your tasks, boost your efficiency, and achieve your goals. With a clean interface and powerful features, it's your ultimate companion for task management.

Key Features

  • Create and manage tasks with priorities.
  • Schedule tasks with date and time.
  • Set reminders and notifications.
  • Pomodoro timer for focused work sessions.
  • Multiple themes and customization options.
  • Dark mode for comfortable nighttime use.
  • Data export/import functionality.
  • User profiles with character avatars.
